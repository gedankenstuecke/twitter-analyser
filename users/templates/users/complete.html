One last step: Uploading your data

Thank you! We have your authorization from Open Humans.

To get your Twitter archive …

  1. Go to your Twitter Data page
  2. Enter your password and request your data
  3. Check your email – you should get a download link for your data!

To upload your tweets…

  1. Downloaded the archive you requested as described above
  2. Open this zipped archive and extract the tweet.js file from it.
  3. Optionally: If your tweet.js file is very big you can make a new zip file only containing that
  4. You can now upload the tweet.js to Open Humans account using the form below.
After all of this your data will be analyzed! Depending on your tweet volume and resulting archive size the upload might take a while.

What's in the tweet.js file I'm supposed to upload?

The file contains all of your tweets along with metadata like geolocation, whether it was a reply and to whom, links etc. It does not contain your direct messages, videos, images or anything else.

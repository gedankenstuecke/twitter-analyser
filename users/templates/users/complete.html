One last step: Uploading your data

Thank you! We have your authorization from Open Humans.

To get your Twitter archive file…

  1. Go to your Twitter account settings
  2. Scroll to the bottom and click "Request your archive"
  3. Check your email – you should soon get a download link!

Once you've downloaded your archive, you can upload it to your Open Humans account using the form below. Then your data will be analyzed! (Depending on your tweet volume and resulting archive size the upload might take a while.)
